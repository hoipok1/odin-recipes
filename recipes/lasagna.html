Lasagna recipe

Yummy lasagna recipe that is easy to make a feed a big family

This lasagna can be frozen in batches so you can take some out and reheat

Ingredients

  • noodle
  • sauce
  • cheese

Steps

  1. First cook your noodles til Al dente
  2. Boil your sauce
  3. Layer the noodle with sauce cheese noodle repeat
  4. Bake in the oven
  5. And there you have it!
