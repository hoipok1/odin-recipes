Mapo Tofu

Silky,spicy mapo tofu that pairs with any rice dish

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Ground pork
  • Green onion
  • Garlic

Seasoning

  • Oyster Sauce
  • Soy Sauce
  • Corn Starch
  • Bean paste

Instructions

  1. Combine all the seasoning in a bowl
  2. Cut garlic and green onion into small pieces
  3. Dice Tofu into 1 in cubes
  4. In a wok fry up the garlic until fragrent, then add in your ground pork until cooked
  5. Add in your sauce and let it simmer, then fold in the tofu gently
  6. Garnish with green onions
